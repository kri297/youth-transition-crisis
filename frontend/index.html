Context & Background

India's Aadhaar system serves as the world's largest biometric digital identity platform, with over 1.3 billion enrolled citizens. For youth aged 5-17 years, periodic biometric updates are mandatory as physical characteristics (fingerprints, iris patterns) change during growth and development.

Research Design

This study employs a quantitative, exploratory research design using secondary data analysis with multi-dimensional statistical framework.

Youth Bio % = (bio_age_5_17 / (bio_age_5_17 + bio_age_17_)) √ó 100
